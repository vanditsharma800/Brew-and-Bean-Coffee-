Brewing Joy in Jaipur

Founded in 2021 in the vibrant lanes of C-Scheme, Brew & Bean was born out of a passion for the perfect roast. We don't just serve coffee; we celebrate the rich heritage of Indian coffee beans, roasted locally to suit the palate of the Pink City.

Our beans are ethically sourced from the hills of Chikmagalur and Coorg, brought straight to Jaipur where they are small-batch roasted every week to ensure maximum freshness.
